About Me

Hi, I'm Margaret Antone, a passionate Graphic Designer & Digital Artist with a love for creating engaging visual content, based out of London, Ontario. As a mother of 3 with a passion for creative design. My work spans various areas including UI/UX design, digital art, and more. I thrive on turning creative ideas into compelling designs.

I want to explore and create in all aspects of life. From creating and designing fun art projects with my children, to bringing my client's ideas to life. I enjoy all aspects of design from start to finish.

I am very focused on giving my clients the end results they imagine!

I have recently completed the INDIGItech program, a specialized initiative by the Indigenous Friends Association aimed at equipping Indigenous individuals with advanced skills in technology, coding, and digital literacy along with digital art. Currently, I am serving as an intern with the Indigenous Friends Association, where I am furthering my education and honing my expertise in Web Development.

Hello I'm Margaret, a creative Graphic Designer based out of London, Ontario. As a mother of 3 with a passion for creative design. I want to explore and create in all aspects of life. From creating and designing fun art projects with my children, to bringing my client's ideas to life. I enjoy all aspects of design from start to finish.

I am very focused on giving my clients the end results they imagine!

I recently completed the INDIGItech program offered by the Indigenous Friends Association, which is designed to equip Indigenous individuals with essential skills in technology, coding, and digital literacy.

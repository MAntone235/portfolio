UX Design Case Study

A group of Mentees from Indigenous Friends Association's Indigitech Program came together to conduct a case study involving access to traditional knowledge.

We came together as a group to define what we thought were the biggest issues with access to Traditional Knowledge.

How Might We

We brainstormed Ideas on solutions to solve this ongoing issue. We decided on ways to gather research, deciding on interviewing participants.

We conducted youth interviews to gage the best way to deliver access. We came to the realization that online would be the best way to reach as many people as possible.

Together we came up with The Indigenous Cultural Bulletin! It is an app that allows communities to post their events, workshops and also teachings.Allowing all these things to be available right at the tip of your fingers. With the world becoming a tech led place this will allow all Indigenous Communities all over the world to add their own page. Allowing Communities to share their own events and also teachings that is relevant to their own teachings and knowledge.

Wall Framing & Subfloor

Drywall & Flooring

Once the framing and sub-floor was completed I then I completed the drywall on the walls and installed the flooring of her choice.

Primer & Finishing Touches

UX Design Case Study

A group of Mentees from Indigenous Friends Association's Indigitech Program came together to conduct a case study involving access to traditional knowledge.

We came together as a group to define what we thought were the biggest issues with access to Traditional Knowledge.

How Might We

We brainstormed Ideas on solutions to solve this ongoing issue. We decided on ways to gather research, deciding on interviewing participants.

We conducted youth interviews to gage the best way to deliver access. We came to the realization that online would be the best way to reach as many people as possible.
